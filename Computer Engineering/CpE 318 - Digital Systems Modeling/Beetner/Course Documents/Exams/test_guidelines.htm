Test Guidelines:
  • Essay and short-answer questions require a complete answer for complete credit. If in doubt, elaborate. A simple "yes" or "no" will rarely get full credit.
  • A question where you include a completely correct answer and some wrong information will not be given full credit. For example, if I ask you to bake a delicious cake and you bake me a delicious cake, you will receive full credit. If you bake me a cake which is edible but not delicious, you will receive partial credit. If you bake me a delicious cake but then put some terrible tasting frosting on it which I didn't ask for, you will only receive partial credit -- the cake is no longer delicious because of the frosting.
  • Good English on essay or short-answer type questions is required - If I can't understand what it is you're trying to say then I can't give you full credit.
  • Feel free to elaborate on any question where you are confused or think your answer requires some explanation. I will take into account what you've said when I grade your test. This includes true/false and multiple choice questions.
  • In general, I will take off points for wrong answers on multiple choice questions. If you would like to try for partial credit, try writing an explanation beside the question rather than guessing.
  • Show your work when possible. Sometimes I am looking for more than just the final answer -- I am looking for the method you used to get it. In those cases, the answer with no work would not receive full credit.
  • If you are running short on time, do your best to outline how you would solve the problem (for partial credit).
  • If you cannot do all of a problem, do what you can. I will give you partial credit.
  • If you are unsure of what I want or am asking, please ask me for advice.
