CpE 311 Lab Exercise 4

Physical level cell design

Due Nov. 14, 2002

Purpose

The purpose of this exercise is to become familiar with physical level cell design using an IC layout editor. Work with your semester project group on this assignment.

Problem Description

Use the schematic driven layout procedure to develop the layout of a complex gate as specified in Lab 3. You may want to work through the SDL tutorial first but that is not a requirement for the lab assignment.  Develop a transistor level schematic for an OAI21X2 gate similar to your design from lab 3 but use the primitives from ADK.  Do NOT include the load capacitor in your layout or you will end up creating a layout for a cell with a very large capacitor included!  You add the 1pF load capacitor after you’re done laying out the cell. Create a layout for your cell using the schematic driven layout process and the ami 0.5 micron technology.

Report

Submit one report per group please.  Your report should include a cover page with the title Lab Assignment No. 4, Physical Level Cell Design, your names, the date, and CPE311.  Include all schematics and simulation output from the tutorial if you work through it.  Include a hardcopy of the OAI21X2 gate schematic, layout, and simulation output.  Include a brief report describing the size of your cell in microns and the propagation delay with a 1pF load.
Include the DRC check report from Chapter 6 and the LVS report from Chapter 7. Do not use any form of binder or protector for your report.  Staple in the upper left corner only.  Your report should not exceed 10 pages, not including schematics and simulation results.  Include all primary source data (data you enter) but do not include any computer generated output other than the hardcopies described above.
